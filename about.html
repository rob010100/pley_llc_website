PLEY - Not Just Another Consultant


Here at PLEY we believe every goal has substance, every person has value, and every dream is worthwhile.


We don't aim to just collab on projects, collect a paycheck and then call it a day. We want to dream together and make a positive impact in our world.

Together we will find the right answers that work for you, your organization, your community, and your budget.



We believe big things often have humble beginnings.

Rob, our founder, got his technological start on a Tandy 1000 in the 1980s. That early fascination with technology never faded, but rather evolved into a lifelong passion for solving problems through technology.


After earning a specialized degree in Information Systems and spending decades in operational management, and now that his own kids are off to college, he decided it was time for something new. Bringing a lifetime of leadership experience and altruistic passions, he returned to his first love: technology.


Now based in Kansas City, MO, Rob founded PLEY in 2024 to serve the small businesses and nonprofits within our community-people and organizations that deserve thoughtful, affordable, and dependable tech support.


Optimizing Technological Solutions

Whether it's building websites from the ground up, managing digital systems, or offering practical consulting, our focus is on building real connections and lasting partnerships.


What's in a name?

Our name, PLEY, was born out of a challenge from a creative solutions workshop: create a four-letter acronym that could inspire in as many ways as possible.


That spirit of creativity and purpose still guides our mission today. As a family based business, we are committed to personalized service and helping our neighbors.


We're also driven by inclusion. Rob’s brother-in-law, who lives with an intellectual disability, has faced ongoing barriers to employment. His experience inspires PLEY's commitment to a hiring model that supports individuals with disabilities.


Community Matters

We’re committed to community partnership, outreach, and strengthening local businesses. If you have a question, whether you're a client, partner, or even a competitor, please don’t hesitate to reach out. Together, we make our community stronger!


Your Time Matters

Contact Us today and within 24 business hours you will hear from us-every, single, time.
