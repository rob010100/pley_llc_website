PLEY - Cookies


Aside from certain monsters, no one in their right mind likes tracking cookies.


PLEY will never use tracking cookies or any type of cookie that violates your privacy.


We value your privacy. We value ours! We believe data privacy is a fundamental human right.


PLEY will never sell your information. We also don't agree with the use of tracking cookies and other methods used to keep tabs on users.


There are future plans to utilize cookies for the use of employee and client sign-ins on our site that will most likely eventually be implemented. These cookies are of the functional nature only and will never track your whereabouts or your online presence.
