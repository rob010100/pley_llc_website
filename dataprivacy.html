PLEY - Data Privacy


Data Privacy is a Human Right


Consumer Data Privacy Policy


Effective Date: 02/23/25

Last Updated: 02/23/25

At PLEY LLC, we prioritize and respect your privacy because we believe data privacy is a fundamental human right. This policy outlines how we handle any information you provide while using our website. Our commitment is simple: to collect only what is necessary, use it responsibly, and never compromise your trust.


What Information We Collect


We collect only the information necessary for the functionality and performance of our website. Examples of such information include:


  • Contact Details: Information voluntarily provided via forms (e.g., name, email address, phone number)
  • Functional Cookies: Essential cookies to facilitate services like client or employee sign-ins (if applicable)
  • Technical Data: Limited technical information like your IP address, browser type, and operating system to optimize the website experience

How We Use Your Information


We use the information we collect exclusively for purposes related to improving your experience on our website, including:


  • To provide requested services or respond to inquiries.
  • To ensure website functionality and security.
  • To comply with legal obligations or address technical issues.

We do not sell, share, or disclose your personal information to third parties for marketing or other purposes.


Retention of Information


We retain your information only for as long as is necessary to fulfill the purpose for which it was collected. Specifically:


  • Personal Information: Retained for up to 6 months from the time of collection, unless required by law to retain it longer.
  • Technical Data: Purged on a rolling basis every 6 months to ensure minimal data retention.

Your Rights and Choices


We believe that you should have control over your information. As such, you have the right to:


  • Request Access: Know what data we’ve collected about you.
  • Request Deletion: Ask for your information to be permanently erased from our systems (unless prohibited by law).
  • Opt-Out: Choose not to provide certain information, understanding this may limit certain website functionalities.

To exercise any of these rights, please Contact Us.


Our Commitment to Security


We implement robust security measures to safeguard your data against unauthorized access, alteration, or loss. These measures include:


  • Encrypted communications via HTTPS.
  • Limited access to your information by authorized personnel only.
  • Routine audits of our systems to ensure compliance with industry best practices.

Cookies and Tracking


We do not use tracking cookies or engage in invasive monitoring practices. Any cookies employed on our website are strictly functional and necessary for:


  • Ensuring smooth navigation.
  • Enabling sign-in or other essential features.

You may disable cookies in your browser settings if you prefer, though some features of the site may not work as intended.


Third-Party Links


Our website may contain links to third-party websites. Please note that we are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of external sites. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party websites you visit.


Changes to This Policy


As technology evolves, so may our privacy practices. Any updates to this policy will be promptly reflected on this page, with the "Last Updated" date revised accordingly. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
