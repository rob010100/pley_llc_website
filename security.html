PLEY - Cybersecurity


Protect Your Website and Your Reputation


Modern websites face more threats than ever before. From bots, bad actors, and employee accidents, all of which are points of vulnerability and exposure.

At PLEY, we help safeguard your online presence through proactive testing, clear assessments, and easy-to-understand action plans.


We'll help you identify potential points of access, eliminate vulnerabilities, and build confidence in your site's security. No jargon or scare tactics involved. Just real-world professional support tailored to your organization and your budget.

Start with Understanding, End with Peace of Mind


Before you can protect your systems, you need to know where you're vulnerable. That's why every PLEY security service starts with a breakdown of your current setup using easy-to-understand terminology.


We don't just scan and walk away. We'll cover these findings with you one on one (or in groups), make recommendations that fit your needs and your budget, and then support this implementation every step of the way.


Our Security Services Include:


  • Website Security Assessment: A top-to-bottom scan of your current website security setup with clear explanations.

  • Deep Security & Database: Advanced probing of backend systems, database exposure, outdated CMS frameworks, and more.

  • Remediation Assistance: Hands-on continuous support fixing the issues we uncover.

  • Ongoing Monitoring: For clients seeking regular scans or quarterly checkups, PLEY has you covered.

We work with HTML/CSS/Java, WordPress, CMS, e-commerce platforms, and more. If you have a web presence, we can help secure it!
